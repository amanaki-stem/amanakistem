UTB, PB Tech and ASA host STEAM Camp in Manawatu

02 Apirl 2021

ASA is humbled to co-host the STEAM Camp in collaboration with 'Using Technology Better (UTB) and PBTechnology. This camp was for children to develop their invention capabilities and let their imaginations run wild as the award- winning tech trainers make it their mission to infuse fun and creativity at every turn. They enjoyed hands-on exploration of cutting-edge digital tools like MBots and drones, and firsthand experience of how technology relates to the real world. This camp showcased limitless career path possibilities, bringing them to life through inspiring career talks from people working in tech- related industries within your own community. It was open to students aged 11-18yrs old.

Who are PB Tecnology and UTB?
PB TECHNOLOGY Founded in 1993 and 100% New Zealand owned, PB Tech is NZ's largest computing and I.T. retailer with stores + service centres throughout the country and a hardworking team of over 600 staff.

USING TECHNOLOGY BETTER (UTB)
Using Technology Better(UTB) provides certified Google, Microsoft and Apple training to organisations in the corporate, government, non-profit and education sectors. The Using Technology Better team of trainers are experts in:
- Ipad management and use including iTunesU, Stop motion animations, iBook creation
- Microsoft environments including Widows in the classroom and O365
- Google Apps for education Including Google Search, Maps, YouTube, Chromebook, and other tools and services
- Google Apps and O365 deployments including deploying both services into schools with synchronised accounts

The ASA vision is to nurture and develop successful Pasifika youth by prioritizing work ethic and normalizing excellence in STEM.
